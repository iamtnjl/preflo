# 22 — KPIs & Success Criteria

> PRD Section: 9, 11 | Priority: Ongoing

## Purpose

Track key metrics and define what success and failure look like.

## Success Milestones

1. **Validated scoring:** 70%+ directional accuracy on 50-keyword benchmark
2. **Organic traction:** 100+ indexed pages, 5,000+ monthly organic visitors
3. **Revenue generation:** $500/month combined AdSense + subscriptions
4. **Calibration loop active:** 50+ reported outcomes, one round of weight adjustments
5. **Sustainable product:** MRR covers infrastructure, 20% retention at 30 days, 75% accuracy

## Failure Criteria (at 6 months)

- Scoring accuracy below 60% despite calibration
- Organic traffic below 1,000 with 200+ indexed pages
- Zero premium subscribers after 3 months of availability
- Consistent "not useful" or "too vague" user feedback
